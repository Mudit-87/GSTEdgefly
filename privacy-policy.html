4. What Data We Collect

4.1 Account and Identity Data

  • Full name and professional designation (CA, CPA, Tax Consultant)
  • Work email address and mobile number
  • Firm name, address, city, and state
  • ICAI membership number (optional, for verification)
  • Billing name and address for invoice purposes
  • Payment reference numbers (UPI/NEFT/RTGS transaction IDs — we do not store card numbers)

4.2 Usage and Technical Data

  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • Features accessed and actions performed within the platform
  • Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • IP address at time of login (stored for security and fraud prevention only)
  • Error logs and crash reports (anonymised)

4.3 GST and Financial Data — Special Category

CRITICAL: GSTR-2B JSON files, GSTR-2A Excel files, Purchase Register files, and any other GST data you upload are processed entirely within your own browser (client-side). These files are never transmitted to, stored on, or processed by GSTEdge AI servers. They exist only in your device's memory during your active session and are permanently deleted when you close the browser tab.

What we do store from your reconciliation sessions:

  • Reconciliation results — ITC totals, invoice counts, match rates, and vendor scores (numerical figures, not raw invoice data)
  • Client profile information you choose to save — firm name, GSTIN, PAN (entered manually by you, not extracted from uploaded files)
  • Monthly tracker data — which months have been reconciled and summary figures
  • Notes you add to client records

We do not store individual invoice numbers, supplier names extracted from uploaded files, raw GSTIN transaction data, or any line-item details from GSTR-2B or Purchase Register files.

4.4 Contact Form and Lead Data

  • Name, email, firm name, city, and phone number submitted via contact forms
  • The subject matter of your enquiry (demo request, access request, GST query)
  • Source of your enquiry (ICAI event, LinkedIn, referral, etc.)

4.5 Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, or biometric data
  • We do not collect credit card or debit card numbers
  • We do not collect bank account details
  • We do not collect any data from your clients directly — only data you choose to enter about them
  • We do not use tracking pixels, third-party advertising cookies, or behavioural profiling tools

6. Data Storage and Localisation GSTN Data Localisation Requirements

All account data, subscription records, and reconciliation summary data stored by GSTEdge AI is hosted exclusively on:

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) — Mumbai, India region (ap-south-1)
  • Application hosting: Vercel — served via CDN with data residency in India

We do not transfer personal data outside India for storage or processing. This is consistent with the GSTN's data localisation requirements and the provisions of the DPDP Act 2023 regarding cross-border data transfers.

In-browser processing: GST files (GSTR-2B, GSTR-2A, Purchase Register) are processed in the user's browser using JavaScript. No data is transmitted to any server during file processing. This architecture is a deliberate design decision to ensure maximum data security and privacy.

10. Security Measures DPDP Act Sec 8(5) — Reasonable Security Safeguards

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.2/1.3 (HTTPS). No data is transmitted over unencrypted connections.
  • Encryption at rest: All data stored in our Supabase database is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Access controls: Row Level Security (RLS) is enforced at the database level — each subscriber can only access their own data. No cross-account data access is possible.
  • In-browser processing: The most sensitive data — raw GST files — never leaves your device. Browser-side processing eliminates the risk of server-side data breaches for uploaded files.
  • Authentication: Per-firm access codes with expiry dates, brute-force protection (5 attempts before 15-minute lockout), and session management via sessionStorage (clears on tab close).
  • No third-party analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking tool that would transmit your usage data to external parties.

Organisational Safeguards

  • Access to production database is restricted to essential personnel only
  • Admin panel access is password-protected and not linked from any public page
  • All team members with data access are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Security incidents will be reported to affected users within 72 hours of discovery
